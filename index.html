Contain

Concrete curbs are an excellent solution for keeping bedding materials in place. They are tall enough in the back to contain the material, yet low enough in the front for easy trimming and maintenance. With a concrete curbing by Cabral Curbing and Landscape, you can keep your garden looking neat and tidy without worrying about mulch, wood chips, or decorative rock spilling out onto your lawn.
